Welcome to ICLR2025

The International Conference on Learning Representations is a premier gathering of researchers working at the intersection of machine learning and representation learning.

About ICLR

The International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR) focuses on the advancement and dissemination of state-of-the-art research in learning representations.

Conference Background

Established in 2013, ICLR has rapidly become a premier venue for the presentation of cutting-edge research on learning representations of data.

Objectives and Scope

ICLR2025 aims to promote high-quality research and applications of learning representations, covering theoretical advancements, methodological innovations, and practical applications.

  • Encourage collaboration and knowledge sharing among researchers and practitioners.
  • Highlight significant breakthroughs in representation learning.
  • Foster mentorship and networking opportunities for emerging researchers.
  • Explore ethical considerations and societal impacts related to the advancements in learning representations.

Travel Information

San Francisco International Airport (SFO) is the nearest major airport, located approximately 13 miles south of the conference venue. Attendees can utilize various transportation methods, including:

  • Airport Shuttle: Multiple shuttle services operate from SFO to downtown San Francisco.
  • Taxis and Rideshares: Taxis and rideshare services like Uber or Lyft are readily available outside the airport terminals.
  • BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit): The BART train provides a convenient and economical means to reach the city.

Local Transportation

San Francisco offers various public transportation options, including:

  • Muni: The San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency operates buses and light rail.
  • Caltrain: For attendees coming from the Peninsula or South Bay, Caltrain provides service to downtown San Francisco.
